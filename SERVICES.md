## 🛠 Available Services

### 1. Deployment & Infrastructure Setup
* **High-Availability Clusters:** Setting up the split-server architecture (UI, DB, and Pollers).
* **Scalable Monitoring:** Configuring and sharding IPMI, SNMP, and ICMP agents for large-scale environments.
* **Cloud & On-Premise Installation:** Full turn-key setup on your hardware or cloud provider.

### 2. Migration & Integration
* **Data Migration:** Seamless transition from "vanilla" RackTables to Datacenter-manager with full data integrity.
* **Third-Party Integrations:** Connecting the platform to your existing Billing, CRM, ERP, or specialized monitoring tools.
* **Custom API Development:** Building bespoke interfaces to automate your specific workflows.

### 3. Custom Feature Development
* Developing unique modules, reports, or plugins specifically for your organization’s requirements.
* Automation scripts for hardware provisioning and network topology management.

### 4. Corporate Training & Support
* Staff training for NOC and OPS teams.
* Ongoing technical support and maintenance contracts.
